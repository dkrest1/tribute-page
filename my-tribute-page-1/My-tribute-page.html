A father with a loving heart ever since my birth

Akintunde Akande (yoruba: Omo dada lode iya eh, oni inu tutu ti o fi irele se gbogbo nkan) he is knowm for is deligent work and most especially a guru in his field as a cook. he is a native of Ogun-state, Nigeria. He is married to Benedict Akande. My father is a loving father that cares for his family and he is pruddent man. He gave birth to five children of which two are boys while the rest are girls. he is so humble that have not seen him for once exchanging words or in arguement with anyone. an he is a man that has the fear of God in him much and he will always go in the path of God hundred if given the chance.
